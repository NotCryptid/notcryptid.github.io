RealityX is a high-performance game engine designed for realistic physics, stunning visuals, and seamless cross-platform development.

Built for ease of use, with simple to use integrations for multiplayer, mod support, Ray Tracing and Virtual Reality.

Designed with the end user in mind. Every game made with RealityX uses top of the line DLSS/FSR Upscaling technology to keep framerates high.

Powered by Swift, for the power of C++, with none of the hassle.
